My favorite Artist: JENNIE

Jennie Kim, known mononymously as Jennie, is a South Korean singer, rapper, songwriter, and actress. She is a member of the popular girl group Blackpink, which debuted in 2016. Jennie was the first member to release solo music with her single "SOLO". She's also made her acting debut under the stage name Jennie Ruby Jane in the 2023 HBO series "The Idol".
